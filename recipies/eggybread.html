Eggy Bread

Foreword:

In this article I will teach you yet another simple but satisfying meal - Eggy Bread!

Ingredients

  • Bread - Cheaper the better
  • 3-4 medium eggs
  • Olive Oil (any cooking oil will work)
  • Milk ~8tbsp

Instructions:

  1. Crack eggs into a container big enough to submerge the bread in
  2. Add in milk and whisk vigoursly until you have a consistent yellow mixture
  3. Bring pan to heat and add oil
  4. Submerge bread in egg mixture, covering thouroughly
  5. Fry both sides evenly
  6. Serve and enjoy, goes great with some bacon, sausages, or beans (or all three!)
